Meet Durandal

Your search for a SPA framework ends here.

Comfortable

We didn't try to re-invent the wheel. Durandal is built on libs you know and love like jQuery, Knockout and RequireJS. There's little to learn and building apps feels comfortable and familiar.
Dive in and enjoy.

Feature-rich

MVC? MVP? MVVM? Yes. Messaging, navigation, modals? Check. Durandal has the features you need to build whatever apps you can imagine;
the apps of today and of tomorrow.
Let your creativity soar.

Versatile

Building an Android phone app? An enterprise LOB targeted at Windows? or a web gaming platform? No matter how large or small the app, it's effortless with Durandal....and we give you the tools to develop on any platform, for any platform.
