Community

Durandal is an open source framework, licensed under the MIT License and hosted on GitHub.

Basic support is provided by the community through our google group. We hope you will join and begin to benefit from the experience of others, as well as share your own knowledge and insite. Additionally, answers to common questions can be found on Stack Overflow. If you find bugs, or wish to make feature requests, feel free to use the github issues system.
